Our Services

Professional Automotive Repair & Maintenance

Complete Automotive Service

ACAL Automotive Group Inc provides professional general maintenance and repair services. Our ASE certified technicians are equipped to handle a wide range of automotive needs.

Oil Changes & Fluid Service

Regular oil changes are essential for engine health. We provide quality oil change services using quality oils and filters. We also service all vehicle fluids including coolant, transmission fluid, brake fluid, and more.

Maintenance Inspections

Regular vehicle inspections help identify potential issues before they become major problems. Our technicians perform comprehensive inspections of your vehicle's major systems and components.

Filter Replacement

We replace air filters, cabin air filters, and fuel filters to keep your vehicle running efficiently and maintain good air quality inside your vehicle.

Brake Service

Proper brake maintenance is crucial for safety. We provide brake inspections, pad replacement, rotor service, and complete brake system service.

Tire Service

We provide tire inspections, rotations, balancing, and replacement services to ensure optimal tire performance and vehicle safety.

Battery Service

Battery testing, replacement, and terminal cleaning services to ensure reliable vehicle starting and electrical system performance.

Belt & Hose Service

Inspection and replacement of serpentine belts, timing belts, radiator hoses, and other critical vehicle components.

Suspension & Steering

We inspect and service suspension components, steering components, and perform wheel alignments to ensure your vehicle handles properly.

Electrical System Service

Diagnosis and repair of electrical system issues including starter, alternator, and lighting system problems.

Cooling System Service

Radiator flushing, thermostat replacement, water pump service, and complete cooling system maintenance.

Transmission Service

Transmission fluid service, filter replacement, and general transmission maintenance to keep your transmission running smoothly.

Diagnostic Service

Professional vehicle diagnostics using state-of-the-art equipment to identify any issues with your vehicle's engine, transmission, or other systems.

Our Professional Certifications

We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of professional certification and expertise:

🏆 ASE A1-7 Certified Technicians

Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) is the gold standard in automotive certification. Our technicians hold comprehensive ASE A1-7 certifications, demonstrating expertise across all vehicle systems:

  • A1: Engine Repair - Diagnosis, service, and overhaul of engines
  • A2: Automatic Transmission/Transaxle - Diagnosis and service
  • A3: Manual Driveline - Drive axles, transfer cases, and driveshafts
  • A4: Suspension & Steering - Alignment and component service
  • A5: Brakes - Complete brake system service
  • A6: Electrical & Electronic Systems - Lighting, charging, and starting systems
  • A7: Heating & Air Conditioning - Climate control systems

These certifications are renewed regularly to ensure our technicians stay current with the latest automotive standards and technology.

❄️ MACS A/C Certified

Mobile Air Conditioning Society (MACS) certification demonstrates our expertise in automotive air conditioning and refrigerant handling. This certification ensures:

  • Proper handling and containment of automotive refrigerants
  • Compliance with EPA regulations and environmental standards
  • Professional diagnosis and repair of A/C systems
  • Correct refrigerant recovery and recycling procedures
  • Knowledge of current A/C technology and repair techniques
  • Proper certification to purchase and handle restricted refrigerants

Our MACS A/C certification allows us to provide comprehensive air conditioning service while maintaining the highest environmental and safety standards.
